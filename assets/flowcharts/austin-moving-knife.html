Austin's Moving Knife Procedure
Phase 1: Referee moves knife across cake from left to right
↓
Decision: Either player calls "STOP" when left piece = 50% to them
↓
Phase 2 Setup: Player who called "STOP" controls two knives. We assume Player 1.
↓
Phase 2: Controlling player (Player 1) moves both knives while maintaining equal middle piece value
↓
Decision: Other player (Player 2) calls "STOP" when they see exact 50-50 division
↓
Phase 3: Random assignment of the two final pieces
Player 1 Gets
Option A: Middle piece OR both flank pieces
Player 2 Gets
Option B: Both flank pieces OR middle piece
↓
Result: Both players receive exactly 50% of their subjective valuation
